NEWS

With over 100 children registered, the first week of APV Kid's Summer Camp 2016 was a 'huge blast', to quote one child! APV Campers have been progressing successfully through their chosen classes of art, music ( Piano, Guitar, Voice ), dramatic acting and nutritional cooking. An exciting array of briskly-paced group activities in the pool, at the playground, during meal time and at our big weekly events, have kept our happy APV campers from even one milli-second of boredom!
